Lesson 3: Active Recovery

Active recovery (movement, nature, breathwork) restores energy faster than passive rest. Build micro-breaks into your day to recharge.

  • List 3-5 activities that help you recharge quickly
  • Schedule two 10-minute active recovery breaks per day
  • Notice the difference in your energy and focus
