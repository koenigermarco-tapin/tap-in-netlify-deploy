How to Improve Your Energy

  • Go to bed 30 minutes earlier for a week.
  • Take a 10-minute walk outdoors at lunch.
  • Try a 2-minute breathing or stretch break every 2 hours.
  • Drink a glass of water first thing in the morning.
  • Limit phone or screen time 1 hour before bed.
  • Notice which people or activities boost or drain your energy—adjust accordingly.
