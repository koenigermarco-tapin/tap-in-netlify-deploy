The Legacy Test

Ask yourself: "If I left tomorrow, what would continue?"

  • Would the team keep performing at this level?
  • Would the culture remain strong?
  • Would the systems keep working?
  • Would anyone be ready to take your place?

If the answer is "no" to any of these, you haven't built a legacy‚Äîyou've built a dependency.
