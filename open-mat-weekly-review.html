Weekly Review Template

15 minutes to extract lessons and plan smarter

Why Weekly Reviews Matter

Without reflection, you're just repeating the same mistakes weekly. With it, you compound learning.

High performers review weekly because:

  • You catch patterns you miss day-to-day (energy drains, time wasters, what actually moves the needle)
  • You celebrate wins (which most people skip, leading to burnout)
  • You course-correct early instead of discovering in December you spent the year on the wrong priorities
  • It creates a feedback loop: try ‚Üí review ‚Üí adjust ‚Üí improve
"We do not learn from experience... we learn from reflecting on experience."
