Why This Works

Most people start their day reactively: check phone, respond to emails, absorb bad news, already stressed by 8am.

This 5-minute routine does the opposite ‚Äî it sets your psychological trajectory for the entire day.

üî¨ The Science

Research shows that morning routines impact:

  • Decision fatigue: Starting with intention preserves willpower for harder choices later
