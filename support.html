Frequently Asked Questions

How do I save my progress?

Your progress is automatically saved to your browser's local storage. As long as you use the same browser and don't clear your browser data, your progress will be saved.

Can I access my progress on multiple devices?

Currently, progress is stored locally on each device. To sync across devices, you would need to export your data (feature coming soon) or create an account.

How do I reset my progress?

You can clear your progress by clearing your browser's localStorage. Go to your browser settings ‚Üí Privacy ‚Üí Clear browsing data ‚Üí Select "Site data" or "Local storage" ‚Üí Clear data.

Is my data private?

Yes! All your data is stored locally on your device. We don't collect or store your personal information on our servers. See our Privacy Policy for more details.

How do I report a bug?

Email us at bugs@tap-in-leadership.com with a description of the issue, what browser you're using, and any error messages you see.

Can I use TAP-IN on mobile?

Yes! TAP-IN is fully responsive and works on mobile devices. You can also install it as a Progressive Web App (PWA) on your home screen.
