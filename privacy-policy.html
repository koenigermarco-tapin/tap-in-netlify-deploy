Privacy Policy

Last Updated: November 30, 2024

1. Introduction

Welcome to TAP-IN Leadership Development Platform ("we," "our," or "us"). We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring you have a positive experience on our website and in using our services.

This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you visit our platform.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Information You Provide

We may collect information that you voluntarily provide to us, including:

  • Assessment responses and results
  • Progress tracking data (belt levels, XP, streaks)
  • Optional account information (if you choose to create an account)

2.2 Automatically Collected Information

We may automatically collect certain information when you use our platform:

  • Browser type and version
  • Device information
  • Usage patterns and preferences
  • Local storage data (for progress tracking)

3. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Provide and improve our services
  • Track your progress through the leadership development program
  • Personalize your learning experience
  • Send you updates about your progress (if you opt in)
  • Analyze usage patterns to improve our platform

4. Data Storage

Local Storage: Your progress, assessment results, and preferences are stored locally in your browser using localStorage. This data remains on your device and is not automatically transmitted to our servers.

No Cookies: We do not use cookies for tracking purposes. Any data storage is done through browser localStorage only.

5. Data Sharing and Disclosure

We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal information to third parties. We may share information only in the following circumstances:

  • With your explicit consent
  • To comply with legal obligations
  • To protect our rights and safety

6. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access: Request access to your personal data
  • Delete: Clear your local storage data at any time
  • Opt-out: Stop using the platform and clear your data

To clear your data, you can clear your browser's localStorage through your browser settings.

7. Data Security

We implement appropriate technical measures to protect your information. However, no method of transmission over the Internet is 100% secure. While we strive to protect your data, we cannot guarantee absolute security.

8. Children's Privacy

Our platform is not intended for children under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

9. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Last Updated" date.

10. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at:

Email: privacy@tap-in-leadership.com
Website: Support Page

11. GDPR Compliance

For users in the European Economic Area (EEA), this platform complies with the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR). Your data is stored locally on your device, and we do not process personal data on our servers without your explicit consent.
