## ğŸ¯ ABANDONMENT REASONS

When a card is abandoned, note the reason:

### Common Abandonment Reasons:
1. **Forbidden Word Used** - Said "you always" (Tier 1)
2. **Vague Language** - Said "sometimes" without specifics (Tier 2)
3. **Power Violation** - Said "you need to" (Tier 3)
4. **Hedge Language** - Said "maybe" or "kind of" (Tier 4)
5. **Character Attack** - Described person vs. behavior
6. **Unclear Request** - R (Request) wasn't specific
7. **Missing Element** - Forgot to include S, B, I, or R
8. **Self-Abandoned** - Player chose to skip rather than attempt

### Why Track Abandonment?

- **Learning tool:** Patterns reveal what to practice
- **Growth metric:** Fewer abandonments over time = mastery
- **Tier readiness:** If Tier 1 causes most abandonments, not ready for Tier 2
- **Coaching opportunity:** Facilitator can target specific struggles
