## üö® COMMON ISSUES & FIXES

**Issue: Low engagement after sign-up**
Fix: DM members personally day 1, 3, 7. Ask specific questions.

**Issue: Nobody booking coaching calls**
Fix: Make it easier. Post Calendly link in every welcome DM + channel.

**Issue: Unlock codes not working**
Fix: Check code format. Ensure app is updated. Regenerate if needed.

**Issue: Members confused about belt progression**
Fix: Create visual roadmap. Pin in #welcome. Explain in every call.

**Issue: Churn after free trial**
Fix: Increase engagement in first 7 days. Book call within 48 hours.

**Issue: Price objections**
Fix: Emphasize ROI. One promotion = 10x the investment. Use testimonials.
