ü•ã The Belt Philosophy

In Brazilian Jiu-Jitsu, you can't skip to black belt. You need to master the fundamentals first - each belt builds on the last.

The same is true for team dysfunction. You can't fix accountability if you don't have commitment. You can't have commitment without conflict. And healthy conflict requires trust as its foundation.

Start where you are. This assessment tells you which belt to train at.

What's Your Starting Point?

Not sure which belt training to start with? This quick assessment will help you find out.

We'll ask you 10 focused questions about your team's dynamics across all 5 dysfunctions. Based on your honest responses, we'll tell you which belt level you're currently at and recommend where to begin your training journey.

No right or wrong answers - just honest reflection about your current team reality.
