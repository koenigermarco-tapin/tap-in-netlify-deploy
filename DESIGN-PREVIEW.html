üìã Design Changes Summary

  • Philosophy Boxes (Standard): Light bg (#f8fafc) with navy border (#1a365d) - readable & consistent
  • Black Belt Wisdom Boxes: Keep dark bg (#1a1a1a) with red border (#b91c1c) - for special moments only
  • Primary Blue: Changed from #2563eb to #1a365d - matches Worker Assessment
  • Dark Navy Sections: Use secondary navy (#334155) for visual variety with red accents
  • Progress Bar: RED gradient (#b91c1c to #dc2626) - TAP-IN brand color
  • Purple (#a78bfa to #7c3aed): BJJ belt progression theme - for emphasis, quotes, special highlights
  • Text Colors: High contrast dark (#1e293b) on light / white (#e2e8f0) on dark

‚úÖ Component Color Guide

  • Standard Philosophy: Light bg (#f8fafc) + navy border (#1a365d) - DEFAULT for BJJ principles
  • Black Belt Wisdom: Dark bg (#1a1a1a) + red border (#b91c1c) - SPARINGLY for profound truths
  • Research Boxes: Primary navy gradient (#1a365d to #334155) - white text, gold emphasis
  • Dark Sections: Secondary navy (#334155 to #1e293b) - for visual variety, red accents
  • Reflection Boxes: Yellow/amber (#fffbeb, #fcd34d) - user prompts/questions
  • Practice Cards: Green (#f0fdf4, #86efac) - actionable items
  • Progress/XP: Red gradient (#b91c1c to #dc2626) - TAP-IN brand
  • Purple (#a78bfa to #7c3aed): BJJ theme - emphasis text, quotes, highlights, belt progression
