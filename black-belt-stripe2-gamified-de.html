üìã The Teaching Framework

Four levels von developing others.

The Development Ladder

1

I Do, Du Watch

Model the behavior. Let them observe mastery in action. Explain dein thinking out loud.

2

I Do, Du Help

Include them in the work. Let them contribute while du lead. Build confidence through participation.

3

Du Do, I Help

Let them lead while du support. Provide guardrails, not answers. Allow productive struggle.

4

Du Do, I Watch

Step back completely. Let them own the outcome. Intervene only for safety. Celebrate their success.
