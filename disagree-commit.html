Disagree & Commit

Strategic decision-making under pressure

How to Play:

  1. Read scenario - Business dilemma with pro/con arguments
  2. Think - 30 seconds silent consideration
  3. Debate - 60 seconds to discuss as a team
  4. Vote - Everyone chooses Agree or Disagree
  5. Consensus - Unanimous = +2 pts each, Majority after 2nd debate = +1 pt
  6. Block tokens - Use 1 of 3 tokens to skip difficult decisions
