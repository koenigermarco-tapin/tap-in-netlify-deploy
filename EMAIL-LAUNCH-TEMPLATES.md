## Final Tips

1. **Personalize the first line** - Reference specific context
2. **Keep it short** - If it doesn't fit on mobile screen, it's too long
3. **Make it easy** - One click to try, one click to respond
4. **Follow up once** - More than twice is annoying
5. **Track everything** - Learn what resonates

**Most important:**

SEND THE EMAIL.

Don't spend 3 days perfecting it.
