Take the Back

Fast-paced accountability game - Own it or abandon it

How to Play:

  1. Card revealed - A "broken thing" appears with 30-second timer
  2. First to claim - Tap "I'VE GOT THE BACK!" to take ownership
  3. Explain it - You have 15 seconds to explain how you'll own it
  4. Group votes - Thumbs up (DELIVERED) or down (ABANDONED)
  5. Score - DELIVERED = +1, ABANDONED = -1. First to 10 wins!
