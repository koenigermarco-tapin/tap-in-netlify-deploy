üìä The Scoreboard Discipline

What gets measured gets managed. What gets displayed gets obsessed over.

Make Results Visible

Great teams have one scoreboard that everyone watches. Not individual dashboards‚Äîa team scoreboard.

Think of a basketball team: everyone knows the score. Everyone knows who needs to step up. There's no hiding.

The Team Scoreboard Test

Ask your team: "What's our score right now?" If people give different answers‚Äîor no answer‚Äîyou don't have a scoreboard. You have chaos.

ü•ã Scoreboards That Work

Good scoreboards are:

  • Simple ‚Äî 3-5 metrics maximum
  • Visible ‚Äî Everyone sees it, all the time
  • Updated ‚Äî Real-time or at least weekly
  • Team-focused ‚Äî Collective metrics, not individual
