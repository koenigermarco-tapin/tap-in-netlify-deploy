The Accountability Framework

1

Clear Commitments

Every meeting ends with explicit commitments: WHO will do WHAT by WHEN. No vague "we should" language.

2

Public Visibility

Commitments are visible to the whole team. Not hidden in 1:1s. What you commit to, everyone sees.

3

Regular Check-ins

Weekly review of commitments. Did you do what you said? No judgment‚Äîjust transparency.

4

Permission to Call Out

Explicit team agreement: we have permission to push each other. It's caring, not criticizing.
